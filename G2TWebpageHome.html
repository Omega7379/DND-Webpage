Getting Started
Hello adventurer, have you come to make your first 5th edition character? Well you've come to the right place! Here you will find the necessary information to get you started as a Player or even the basics to being a Dungeon Master. I personally highly recommend that you get a hold of Player's Handbook (PHB), Dungeon Master Guide (DMG), and the Essentials Kit! Through this page I will be making my own character as an example, however results will vary from person to person.
